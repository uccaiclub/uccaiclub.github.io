Welcome to the UCC AI club

What Is AI?

AI or Artificial Intelligence refers to software technoligeis that make a robot or computer act and think like a human.

Examples

  • Siri and Alexa
  • Self Driving Car
  • Facial Recognition
  • Uber
  • Google Translate

Our goals

We want to introduce some fun activites with coding to UCC students.

We are a group of UCC students who are interested in STEM, coding, machine learning and web design!

What we do

We meet every week to learn to code and build projects. We also invite guest speakers from AI or coding companies to speak at our club. To get started, we focus on three areas.

  • Getting Comfortable with coding(building a website using HTML CSS)
  • Introduction to STEAM: building robots.
  • Learn Basic Python
