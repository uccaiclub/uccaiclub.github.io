Welcome to the UCC AI Club

What Is AI?

AI or Artificial Intelligence refers to software that makes a robot or computer act and think like a human.

Examples:

  • Siri and Alexa
  • Self Driving Car
  • Facial Recognition
  • Uber
  • Google Translate

Our goals

We want to introduce some fun activites to UCC students: STEM, coding, machine learning and web design!

What we do

We meet every week to learn to code and build projects. We also planning to have guest speakers from AI or coding companies to speak at our club. To get started, we are focusing on three areas:

  • Getting Comfortable with coding(building a website using HTML CSS)
  • Building and programming robots
  • Learn Basic Python
